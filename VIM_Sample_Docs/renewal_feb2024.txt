SECTION 1 — SERVICES
-------------------------------------------------------------------
Twilio agrees to continue providing the following services:

  1.1  SMS Messaging API — Programmatic outbound and inbound SMS.
  1.2  Voice API — Programmable voice and conferencing.
  1.3  WhatsApp Business API — Notifications and two-way messaging.
  1.4  Twilio Verify — OTP and phone number verification service
       (new addition to service bundle).

SECTION 3 — SERVICE LEVELS
-------------------------------------------------------------------
  3.1  Service Availability
       Twilio will use commercially reasonable efforts to maintain
       availability of the SMS Messaging API and other services
       covered under this Agreement.

  3.2  Support Response Times
       Severity P1 (full outage)  : 2-hour response
       Severity P2 (degraded)     : 8-hour response
       Severity P3 (minor)        : Next business day response

  3.3  Scheduled Maintenance
       Twilio may perform scheduled maintenance during low-traffic
       windows with 48 hours advance notice to Customer.

  3.4  Incident Communication
       Twilio will make reasonable efforts to notify Customer of
       significant service incidents via the Twilio Status Page
       at status.twilio.com.
