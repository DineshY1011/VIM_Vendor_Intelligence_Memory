SECTION 1 — SERVICES
-------------------------------------------------------------------
Twilio agrees to provide the following communication services to
Customer under the terms of this Agreement:

  1.1  SMS Messaging API — Programmatic outbound and inbound SMS
       delivery via Twilio's global carrier network.

  1.2  Voice API — Programmable voice calls, IVR, and conferencing.

  1.3  WhatsApp Business API — Customer notifications and two-way
       messaging via the WhatsApp channel.

-------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2 — PRICING
-------------------------------------------------------------------
  2.1  SMS Rate
       Agreed price per outbound SMS (domestic India):
       USD $0.0075 per SMS

       This rate is locked for the full term of this Agreement
       (March 1, 2022 through June 1, 2024) and shall not be
       increased without a minimum of 60 days written notice to
       Customer.

  2.2  Voice Rate
       USD $0.013 per minute (outbound), USD $0.0085 per minute
       (inbound). Rates locked for contract term.

  2.3  WhatsApp Rate
       USD $0.005 per conversation (24-hour session window).

  2.4  Volume Commitment
       Customer commits to a minimum monthly spend of USD $2,000.
       Spend below this threshold will be invoiced at the minimum
       commitment amount.

  2.5  Payment Terms
       Net-30 from invoice date. Late payments accrue interest at
       1.5% per month.

-------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 3 — SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)
-------------------------------------------------------------------
  3.1  Uptime Guarantee
       Twilio guarantees 99.95% monthly uptime for the SMS
       Messaging API. Downtime is calculated as minutes of
       total service unavailability per calendar month.

  3.2  SLA Credit Schedule
       If Twilio fails to meet the 99.95% uptime commitment,
       Customer is entitled to service credits as follows:

         Uptime 99.50% – 99.94%  →  10% credit of monthly invoice
         Uptime 99.00% – 99.49%  →  25% credit of monthly invoice
         Uptime below 99.00%     →  50% credit of monthly invoice

       Credits must be claimed by Customer within 30 days of the
       incident. Credits will be applied to the following invoice.

  3.3  Support Response Times
       Severity P1 (full outage)  : 1-hour response, 4-hour resolution
       Severity P2 (degraded)     : 4-hour response, 24-hour resolution
       Severity P3 (minor)        : Next business day response

  3.4  Incident Notification
       Twilio will notify Customer of any P1 or P2 incidents via
       email within 30 minutes of detection. Post-incident reports
       will be delivered within 5 business days.

-------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 4 — TERM AND RENEWAL
-------------------------------------------------------------------
  4.1  Initial Term
       This Agreement is effective from March 1, 2022 and continues
       through June 1, 2024 ("Initial Term").

  4.2  Renewal
       This Agreement will auto-renew for successive 12-month periods
       unless either party provides 90 days written notice of
       non-renewal prior to the end of the then-current term.

  4.3  Price Lock on Renewal
       Any pricing changes at renewal require 60 days advance written
       notice. Customer retains the right to terminate without penalty
       within 30 days of receiving notice of a price increase.
